Why BIKERIDER?

Cycling with BikeRiders in a group is a good option to take a look and start calibrating your roads. And that's how you can get to know a place better, accompanied by other people who are doing the same as you. Also, when riding in a group the tendency is to keep up, and that means that depending on your position with respect to whoever is in front of you, the same push will cause you to use less energy when pedaling. Another advantage is security. In a group it is easier to go further and to unpopulated places.
